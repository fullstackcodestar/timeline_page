More about Simon-Kucher

Where it all began: Our founders' vision laid the foundation for everything we stand for today.

  • 1985 – Establishing our roots

    Simon-Kucher was founded in Bonn, Germany in 1985 as a university spinoff by Prof Dr Hermann Simon, Dr Eckhard Kucher, and Dr Karl-Heinz Sebastian. Their vision: Use scientific methods to address real-life business challenges and help companies grow. The company was led by Hermann Simon as CEO from 1995 until 2009.

  • 2009 - Dr Georg Tacke and Dr Klaus Hilleke take the reins

    Founder Prof Dr Hermann Simon stepped down in 2009 as CEO and remained Chairman. He was succeeded by co-CEOs Dr Georg Tacke and Dr Klaus Hilleke. In 2016, Georg Tacke took on the role as sole CEO. When he retired at the end of 2019, Tacke handed over a global company with 39 offices in 26 countries.

  • 2020 – Andreas von der Gathen and Mark Billige elected as CEOs

    Mark Billige and Dr. Andreas von der Gathen have played a pivotal role in Simon-Kucher’s evolution. Over their five years as co-CEOs, they have transformed the firm from the "best-kept secret" in consulting to a recognized global leader in pricing and growth consulting.

  • 2025 – Joerg Kruetten and Dr. Gunnar Clausen appointed Co-CEOs

    In 2025, Joerg Kruetten and Dr. Gunnar Clausen became co-CEOs. With deep expertise in strategy, pricing, and business transformation, they bring decades of leadership and innovation, ensuring Simon-Kucher continues driving success for clients worldwide.

Value Pricing

Published 1997, Springer

Simon contributed to the advancement of value pricing, arguing that pricing should be determined by the customer’s perception of value, not the cost of production. He pointed out that many companies lose significant revenue because they underprice their products, failing to capture the full value they deliver to customers. His research showed that companies can substantially increase profitability by aligning prices with the perceived value customers receive from a product or service. Simon-Kucher has continued to apply value-based pricing strategies in real-world scenarios, proving their effectiveness across industries....

Monnetizing Innovation

Published 1998, Springer

The concept of Monetizing Innovation was formalized in our firm's 2016 book, Monetizing Innovation, authored by partners Madhavan Ramanujam and Georg Tacke. This framework highlighted the importance of integrating pricing strategy into the innovation process from the very beginning. This concept encourages businesses to integrate pricing and customer value research early in the product development cycle, ensuring that products are designed to meet market needs and maximize revenue potential....
